Privacy & Data

Current site behavior:

No server-side tracking
The repository-hosted page and the Unity WebGL build run client-side. There is no server-side collection of personal data by default.
Local browser requests
Your browser will request the build assets (WASM, data files, JS) from GitHub Pages when you open the build; those requests are handled by GitHub's infrastructure.
Third-party services
If analytics or third-party services are added later, they may collect usage data. Any such additions will be documented and options for opting out will be provided where possible.

Ads & Monetization (possible in future)

I may introduce advertising or third-party monetization later. If/when I add ads:

  • I will clearly note it on this page.
  • Third-party ad providers may set cookies or collect usage data according to their policies.
  • If you prefer not to be tracked, use browser privacy tools (ad blockers, tracker blockers) or browser settings.
